Snapme - Data Deletion Instructions

Data Deletion Instructions

The following instructions will guide you through the process of deleting your data from the Snapme web application.

1. Log in to your account on the Snapme web application.

2. Navigate to the Settings page.

3. Click on the "Delete Account" button.

4. Confirm the deletion of your account by entering your account password.

5. Your account, along with all of your associated data, will be permanently deleted.

Note: Deletion of your account and data is permanent and cannot be undone. We recommend downloading any data you wish to save before proceeding with deletion.
